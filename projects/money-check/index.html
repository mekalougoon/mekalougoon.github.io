Brief

To develop a single-function mobile app that improves the day-to-day life of another person, or a group of people you know. The objective of the app is to simply improve ONE particular aspect of their daily activities. This app must then be prototyped using Figma.

I decided to make a budgeting and savings app, made on Figma. Features a full case study from site maps, to experience flows and so on. My first figma project.
